About Us

The Masaka Social Center was established to support our community through various initiatives that uplift and empower. Our goal is to provide resources and opportunities for all. Services Offered.

  • Meeting space.
  • Recreational facilities.
  • Food and beverages services.
  • Support services.

    • Our Vision

    A vibrant community hub where people feel empowered,connected and supported.

    Our Roles

  • Reach out to community members to promote services,programs and events.
  • Manage the social center's facilities including maintanance,repairs and rentals.
  • Develop and implement programs and activities for various age groups and interests.

    • Our Mission

    "To offer comprehensive support services,resources and advocacy to vulnerable populations and self sufficiency"
